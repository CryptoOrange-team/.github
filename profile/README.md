**CryptoOrange** is a blockchain development team focused on smart contract engineering, DeFi protocols, and on-chain security. We build secure, gas-optimized, and formally verified protocols across EVM, Solana, and Move ecosystems.

---

### Focus

| Smart Contracts | DeFi | Security |
|:--|:--|:--|
| ERC Standards and EIPs | AMM and Liquidity | Auditing and Fuzzing |
| Account Abstraction | Staking and Yield | Formal Verification |
| Cross-Chain Bridges | Lending Protocols | MEV Mitigation |
| Layer 2 Rollups | Tokenomics Design | Bug Bounty Programs |
